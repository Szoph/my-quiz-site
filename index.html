You are a Fire type Pokémon!

Fire type Pokémon are very driven and can stay focused. They have a certain aura that makes them very popular even if they avoid attention. They deal with problems head on and can struggle with issues outside their interests.

You are a Water type Pokémon!

Water type Pokémon are adaptable and well balanced. They are more likely to "go with the flow". They love water or the sense of calm water brings and can work well with virtually anyone.

You are a Grass type Pokémon!

Grass type Pokémon form special relationships with their environments. They love nature and living things, and are good at finding solutions in a creative way.

You are a Normal type Pokémon!

Normal type Pokémon may be common but they are also very diverse. They may enjoy and relate to extreme oddities and/or have a fondness for domestic life. They may struggle to stay focused and change their minds often.

You are a Ghost type Pokémon!

Ghost type Pokémon are mischievous and very funny (even if their sense of humour is very dark). They are interested in unusual history. They rarely deal with problems in a typical way and manay issues don't seem to bother them.
